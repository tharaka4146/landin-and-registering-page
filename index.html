Why DevZone

DevOps is not a Goal, But a never-ending process of continual improvement. DevOps simply adds the idea that small, cross-functional teams should own the entire delivery process from concept through user feedback and production monitoring. Set of practices that leads to the combination of software development and IT operations. Do you know where to get this knowledge from, to work in the industry? Well, the DevZone will help you with it, as it focuses on the IT Infrastructure aspects and provides you with the basic knowledge about DevOps that will give you great benefits in the upcoming future. Stay tuned with us for a whole new experience with DevOps, the one most responsive to change.
